About Me

I’m currently a Data Engineer based in Rochester NY. I graduated from SUNY Brockport with a B.S. in Computer Science where I developed a deep passion for programming, primarily learning and developing in Java. Since then, I’ve chased that passion by working to become a better developer everyday, while contributing to my company by implementing data solutions for a variety of meaningful and interesting projects.

I have a love for building useful things through programming, whether that be scripts, websites, dashboards, or anything in-between. Learning new technologies is a hobby of mine, and my recent projects have given me experience using Javascript, React, Python, Django, R, HTML & CSS.

When I’m not glued to a computer I enjoy reading, running, and playing basketball. I listen to podcasts while I run, so if you have any good recommendations send them my way!
